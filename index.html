Kaggle Dataset Link: Students Performance in Exams


Overview of Dataset: This dataset provides a comprehensive overview of the academic performance of 1,000 students across three subjects: Math, Reading, and Writing. It includes detailed information on each student's gender, race/ethnicity group, parental level of education, lunch type, and test preparation course status. Finally, it records their scores in Math, Reading, and Writing.
